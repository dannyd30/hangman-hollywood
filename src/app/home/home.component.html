Here is how the game works...

  1. It's a two player game (Duh..!)
  2. One player decides a hollywood movie name, which the other play has to guess and find out
  3. The vowels (a,e,i,o,u) and any non-alphabetic character is shown, while the rest of the alphabets are left as blank
  4. The guessing player has a total of 9 (H,O,L,L,Y,W,O,O,D) chances to get all the alphabets
  5. If you guess it within 9 turns.. You get two points!!
  6. If you cannot guess the movie name, the other guy gets 1 point!!
  7. Highest score at the end of the game wins.. Again DUH!!!
